Go Shala Seva: Honoring and Protecting Cows

Go Shala Seva, the service dedicated to the welfare of cows, reflects a deep-rooted cultural and spiritual respect for Gau Mata. We support Go Shalas in ensuring that these revered animals receive proper care, nourishment, and protection.

At Sri Vidya Charities, Go Shala Seva is one of the vital programs. We assist in providing resources for the upkeep and protection of cows in different Go Shalas, helping to preserve India's tradition of respecting and revering these gentle beings. Through this seva, we contribute towards sustainability, compassion, and the well-being of all living creatures.

Why Go Shala Seva Matters?


Nourishment & Care: We provide essential fodder, medicines, and support to ensure cows are well-nourished and healthy.


Sustainable Living: Go Shalas promote sustainable agricultural practices, and we support the use of cow dung and urine for organic fertilizers and pest control.


Preservation of Native Breeds: Our support helps conserve native cow breeds, preserving biodiversity and enriching rural livelihoods.
