Sanskrit Samrakshana: Preserving the Language of the Gods

Sanskrit, often referred to as the "language of the gods," is a vital part of India's rich spiritual and cultural heritage. At Sri Vidya Charities, we dedicate efforts to preserve and promote Sanskrit through various initiatives that support scholars, education, and research in this timeless language.

We believe that Sanskrit is more than just a language; it is the thread that connects ancient wisdom to modern life. By nurturing and expanding the study and use of Sanskrit, we ensure the preservation of Indiaâ€™s most profound teachings and the continued dissemination of its knowledge.

Why Sanskrit Samrakshana Matters?


Cultural Heritage: Sanskrit is the foundation of India's spiritual, literary, and scientific knowledge.


Spiritual Wisdom: It holds the teachings of the Vedas, Upanishads, and other sacred scriptures.


Global Significance: Sanskrit has influenced languages worldwide and continues to offer insights into various disciplines.
