Srividya Charities Trust, is a registered non-profit private Trust, based at Halasuru, Bengaluru, registered under no HLS-4-00324-2022-23. The members of Srividya Charities Trust have been involved in selfless service to the needy for the past 4 decades.

Embracing the concept of “Vasudeiva Kutumbhakam” (“The World is One Family”), Srividya Charities Trust aims to promote happiness, peace and higher consciousness to all involved.

Annadanam (Narayan Seva)

Annadhaanam, as per Vedic Scriptures is considered the greatest of all “Dhaanams”. Annam (Food) is equated to Almighty God as per our Vedic scripts. Annadhanam to the needy, deserving is considered to be auspicious & is a charity of high order.

Vidyadanam

Vidyadanam, the gift of education, empowers individuals with knowledge and wisdom, fostering self-reliance and societal growth. By supporting this seva, we help preserve our rich cultural heritage and ensure a brighter future for generations to come.

Vedapatashala

Supporting Vedapathashalas ensures the preservation and propagation of Vedic knowledge, nurturing future generations of scholars.
Through this seva, we safeguard the timeless wisdom that forms the foundation of Sanatana Dharma.

Goshala- Go Grasa Seva

Every time there used to be an incident with baby Shri Krishna when Kansa sent various demons to kill him, Yashoda Maa and the people of Gokul would take him to the cowshed to get rid of any problems over Kanhaiya and to protect him.
Following on from this, Pujya Bhaishri explains the importance of the holy mother cow and cow dung.

Sanskrit language and Scriptures

Supporting Vedapathashalas preserves the ancient wisdom of Vedic teachings, nurturing spiritual growth and cultural heritage. By contributing to this seva, we ensure that these timeless traditions are passed down to future generations, enriching their lives and communities.
