WELCOME TO SRIVIDYA CHARITIES TRUST ®

Srividya Charities Trust, is a registered non–profit private Trust, based at Halasuru, Bengaluru, registered under no HLS–4–00324–2022–23. The members of Srividya Charities Trust have been involved in selfless service to the needy for the past 4 decades.

Srividya Charities Trust is an endeavour to present an opportunity to like–minded people to channelise their efforts, resources, and donation for the benefit of the Society.

Embracing the concept of “Vasudeiva Kutumbhakam” (“The World is One Family”), Srividya Charities Trust aims to promote happiness, peace, and higher consciousness to all involved.

Aradhana Mahotsava 2024

15th September meeting with the Dharmic Organisations

SVCT organised a meeting by inviting the Dharmic and Satsang organisations of Bengaluru, to appraise them about the conduct of Sahasra Bhojana Maha Yagnam 2025 scheduled from 7th July 2025. The meeting on 15th September was well attended. About 11 Satsang Organization’s representatives attended the meeting. The guests were appraised about the Significance of Sahasra Bhojana Maha Yagnam, it’s relevancy of being performed by SVCT. A presentation was organised for the guests to have a Birds’ eye view. This was well received and appreciated by the august audience present. An Appeal was made soliciting moral, physical and volunteer support for SBMY 2025, which was reciprocated by the organisations. The meeting concluded with a Vote of Thanks followed by lunch. In all 35 members attended the meeting. A group photo was taken on the occasion and shared below. The proceedings of the meeting was published in Dina Malar Daily on 17th September 2024.
